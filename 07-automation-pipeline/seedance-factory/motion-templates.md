# Seedance Motion Templates — M1 through M6

6 locked motion patterns for image-to-video. Pick one per clip based on `format-cheatsheet.md`. Each template lists the Seedance prompt-string, ideal-still-input-spec, output-format-recommendations, and when-to-use guidance.

> **Source still:** all motion templates expect a Higgsfield Soul-2 render (Soul-ID `948dbc10-ba8d-406c-b771-e15610ae8674`) from Set A or Set C. The still is the identity anchor — Seedance only adds motion.

---

## M1 — Push-in slow

**Motion:** Camera dollies forward 10% over the duration. Subject stays anchored center-frame; background slowly enlarges + softens. Subtle ambient particles (dust, light flecks) drift through frame.

**Duration:** 6s
**Frame rate:** 30fps (or 24fps for LinkedIn cinematic)
**Output formats:** 16:9 (1920×1080), 9:16 (1080×1920), 4:5 (1080×1350)

### Seedance prompt-string

```
Slow cinematic push-in over 6 seconds. Camera dollies forward
approximately 10% from start to end. Subject stays anchored at
center-frame, gaze direction unchanged, mouth closed throughout.
Background subtly enlarges and softens. Ambient atmospheric
particles (fine dust motes, light flecks) drift slowly through
frame. Lighting unchanged. Color grade unchanged (teal-orange brand
grade preserved). No subject head-rotation, no expression change,
no lip-movement.
```

### Ideal still-input spec

- Set A portrait with a centered subject (A01 productiehal arms-crossed, A03 blueprint-tafel leaning, A05 klant-locatie three-quarter, A07 podium looking-at-camera)
- Subject mouth CLOSED in still (motion will not animate lips, but starting closed is safer)
- Background depth — push-in needs visible parallax cue (out-of-focus machinery, doorway, etc.)
- No active hand-gesture in still (still hands = stable hands across motion)

### When to use

- BOFU LinkedIn 16:9 authority ads
- Lead-magnet hero loops
- "Who am I" intro clips (5–6s pull-the-viewer-in)

## M2 — Slow zoom-out reveal

**Motion:** Camera pulls back 15–20% over duration, revealing more of the setting. Subject stays in same relative position; viewer sees more environmental context emerge.

**Duration:** 7s
**Frame rate:** 30fps
**Output formats:** 16:9 (1920×1080), 4:5 (1080×1350)

### Seedance prompt-string

```
Slow cinematic zoom-out reveal over 7 seconds. Camera pulls back
approximately 15-20% from start to end, revealing additional
environmental context (production hall, blueprints, factory
corridor, monitors). Subject remains in same relative center
position, gaze direction unchanged, mouth closed, posture stable.
Lighting unchanged. Color grade unchanged (teal-orange brand grade
preserved). Subtle environmental motion in background (workers in
soft motion blur far behind, light shimmer on machinery). No
subject head-rotation, no expression change.
```

### Ideal still-input spec

- Set A portrait with a tight or medium framing where pulling back will reveal context (A02 werkplaats examining-blueprint, A04 monitor-setup pointing, A06 conferentie in-conversation, A09 magazijn-rek holding-laptop)
- Setting must have visible depth/extra context that's worth revealing
- Subject hands stable (on desk / blueprint / laptop — locked positions)

### When to use

- LinkedIn case-study 16:9 ads ("here's the work environment")
- 4:5 feed-large case-study reveal
- "Behind-the-scenes" Stories / Reels intros

## M3 — Parallax pan

**Motion:** Camera pans laterally 5–8% (left-to-right or right-to-left). Foreground (subject) and background separate visually due to depth — true parallax effect.

**Duration:** 6s
**Frame rate:** 30fps
**Output formats:** 9:16 (1080×1920), 4:5 (1080×1350)

### Seedance prompt-string

```
Cinematic parallax pan over 6 seconds. Camera moves laterally
approximately 5-8% (left-to-right or right-to-left, choose direction
that flatters the source still composition). Foreground subject and
background separate visually due to depth — true parallax effect,
background drifts faster than foreground. Subject remains in frame,
gaze direction unchanged, mouth closed, posture stable. Lighting
unchanged. Color grade unchanged (teal-orange brand grade preserved).
No subject head-rotation, no expression change.
```

### Ideal still-input spec

- Set A portrait with strong foreground/background separation (A01 productiehal arms-crossed, A05 klant-locatie three-quarter, A08 fabrieks-vloer walking-away)
- Background must have visual texture that registers when it drifts (machinery, racking, corridor walls)
- Subject body angle: three-quarter or front-facing works best

### When to use

- TOFU Reel 9:16 hooks (instant motion = thumb-stop)
- Feed-large 4:5 cold-traffic ads
- Carousel video preview-clips

## M4 — Subject-shift gaze

**Motion:** Gaze direction shifts naturally over the clip (e.g., subject looks from off-camera right → toward lens). Body doesn't move; only eye direction + subtle head-angle change.

**Duration:** 5s
**Frame rate:** 30fps
**Output formats:** 1:1 (1080×1080), 4:5 (1080×1350)

### Seedance prompt-string

```
Subtle subject gaze-shift over 5 seconds. Subject's eyes shift
naturally from initial gaze direction to a new gaze direction
(if starting off-camera, end on-camera; if starting on-camera, end
slightly off to suggest thoughtful shift). Head angle adjusts
minimally (5-10 degrees max) to support the eye movement. Mouth
remains closed throughout, NO lip-movement, NO expression change
to smile or grin. Body posture, hands, wardrobe all locked. Lighting
unchanged. Color grade unchanged (teal-orange brand grade preserved).
NO talking, NO mouth-open frame.
```

### Ideal still-input spec

- Set A portrait with subject NOT making direct eye contact OR with subject in three-quarter angle (A02 werkplaats examining-blueprint, A06 conferentie in-conversation, A10 kantoor-deurpost conferring)
- Hands stable in still
- The gaze shift creates engagement without requiring body motion

### When to use

- MOFU 1:1 Feed engagement ads
- 4:5 portrait+stat ads where you want subject to "notice" viewer
- Quote-card video versions (subject reacts to quote text)

## M5 — Tool-in-hand

**Motion:** Subtle motion of the tool/object the subject is holding — laptop slowly closing, pencil tapping, blueprint corner lifting in air-current. Subject body stays still; only the held-object animates micro-movement.

**Duration:** 6s
**Frame rate:** 30fps
**Output formats:** 4:5 (1080×1350), 1:1 (1080×1080)

### Seedance prompt-string

```
Subtle tool-in-hand motion over 6 seconds. The object the subject is
holding (laptop / pencil / blueprint / portfolio) animates with micro-
movement appropriate to the object: laptop lid drifts very slightly,
pencil tip taps blueprint once, blueprint corner lifts in soft air-
current, portfolio shifts marginally. Subject body, head, gaze,
mouth, expression all locked — only the tool moves. Lighting
unchanged. Color grade unchanged. NO body movement, NO head-turn,
NO talking, NO smile.
```

### Ideal still-input spec

- Set A portrait with subject holding/interacting with a tool (A02 werkplaats examining-blueprint, A03 blueprint-tafel leaning-on-desk, A09 magazijn-rek holding-laptop)
- The tool must be clearly visible in frame
- Subject pose locked + stable (face/body unchanged across the clip)

### When to use

- MOFU/BOFU 4:5 portrait+stat ads (Wouter "actively working" while stat overlays load)
- "Live demo" intro clips for course pitches
- Lead-magnet PDF preview loops

## M6 — Environmental motion

**Motion:** Subject is locked. Background animates (workers passing in soft blur, machine spinning slowly far back, light flickering on monitor in distance, dust motes drifting). Subject is the "still rock" — environment is alive.

**Duration:** 8s
**Frame rate:** 30fps
**Output formats:** 1:1 (1080×1080), 4:5 (1080×1350), 9:16 (1080×1920)

### Seedance prompt-string

```
Environmental motion over 8 seconds with subject completely locked.
Background elements animate: hi-vis-vest workers pass slowly in soft
motion blur far behind, machinery rotates or pulses slowly in deep
background, monitor screen flickers softly, dust motes drift through
shafts of light, factory ambient haze breathes. Subject body, head,
gaze, mouth, expression, hands, wardrobe all completely locked — the
subject is a still rock, the environment is alive around them.
Lighting unchanged. Color grade unchanged (teal-orange brand grade
preserved). NO subject motion of any kind.
```

### Ideal still-input spec

- Set A portrait with rich background motion potential (A01 productiehal arms-crossed, A05 klant-locatie three-quarter, A08 fabrieks-vloer walking-away — paradoxically works because background workers can pass while Wouter is mid-frame)
- Background depth-of-field shallow enough that motion is implied but not jarring
- Subject in a stable pose (no mid-step gestures)

### When to use

- TOFU 1:1 Feed typography ads (Wouter as "still authority" while world moves)
- 9:16 Reel hooks where the subject's stillness IS the impact
- "Working in the chaos" employer-brand vibes

## Identity-string (append to every Seedance prompt)

Append this block to ANY of the 6 motion-template prompt-strings before submitting:

```
IDENTITY PRESERVATION (locked across all frames):
- Light grey wool blazer — no color drift to navy/black/brown
- White shirt + black knit tie — no pattern shift, no silk shift
- Round-frame matte black acetate glasses — always present, NOT wire,
  NOT rectangular, NOT missing
- Salt-and-pepper hair side-parted neat — no style change
- 3-day salt-and-pepper stubble — no clean-shaven, no full beard
- Mouth closed or barely-parted — NEVER broad smile, NEVER toothy grin,
  NEVER open-mouth talking pose
- Real skin texture preserved — NO beauty-filter morph mid-clip
- Hand anatomy stable — NO extra fingers, NO morphing hands across
  frames

If any of the above drifts between frame 1 and the final frame,
reject the clip.
```

## Selection logic (quick decision tree)

```
Need attention-grabbing TOFU motion in vertical?
  → M3 Parallax pan (9:16 Reel) or M6 Environmental (1:1 Feed)

Need authority push (BOFU LinkedIn)?
  → M1 Push-in slow (16:9 sponsored)

Need case-study reveal?
  → M2 Slow zoom-out (16:9 LinkedIn) or M5 Tool-in-hand (4:5)

Need engagement micro-motion (MOFU)?
  → M4 Subject-shift gaze (1:1 Feed)

Need "still authority + chaos" Mood?
  → M6 Environmental (any format)
```

See `format-cheatsheet.md` for the full motion × format × tier matrix.
