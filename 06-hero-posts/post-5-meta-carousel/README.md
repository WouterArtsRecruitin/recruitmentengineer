## 7. Hook timeline (carousel-critical)

Carousel sweep-tracking is anders dan video — IG meet "complete-to-end" + "save-rate".

| Slide | Function | Critical |
|-------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Visual stop | Hook — moet thumb-stop in eerste 1.5 sec on feed |
| 2-6 | Modules | Bouwt waarde + bewijs op |
| 7 | DOEL spec-sheet | Stop-moment voor "saver"-personality |
| 8 | €519k case | Emotionele peak (BIG number = save-trigger) |
| 9 | CTA-prep | Bouwt swipe-naar-10 momentum |
| 10 | Download-CTA | Conversie-point |

**Sweep-target:** 50%+ swipe-to-end (industry avg = 30-40% voor 10-slide carousels).
**Save-target:** 8% save-rate (high-value lead-magnet preview).
