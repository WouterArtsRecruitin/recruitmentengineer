## Diagnostic thresholds

If at any point during Day 1-5:

| Metric | Threshold | Diagnosis | Action |
|--------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Post views | < 2.000 | Algorithm suppression | Engage earlier on other posts (within 60min of publish) |
| New followers | < 10 (cumulative) | Headline / banner unclear | Revise headline or swap banner |
| PDF downloads | < 20 (cumulative by Day 3) | CTA too weak in posts | Make CTA more explicit, add hard-link in body |
| Pipedrive leads | < 2 (cumulative by Day 3) | Landing page conversion issue | A/B test hero copy, check form flow on mobile |
