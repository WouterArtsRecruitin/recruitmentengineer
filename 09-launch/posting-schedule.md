## Per-post engagement protocol

Every published post:

1. **Publish** at scheduled time (don't drift more than 15 min)
2. **Comment 1** within 60 seconds — link to landing page
3. **Comment 2** at +2h — engagement question to fuel comment thread
4. **Reply to ALL comments** within 4h (algorithm-boost)
5. **DM thank-you** to anyone who shares or comments meaningfully
6. **3 reactions** on other posts in your niche within 60min after publish

**Power-move:** when someone reacts to your post → check their profile → like their latest post with a meaningful comment. ~30% boost in mutual reach.

## Day-of execution (per post)

```
T-30min  → final read-through, fix typos
T-5min   → open LinkedIn web + mobile in parallel (mobile to verify)
T-0      → publish
T+0:01   → post Comment 1 (landing link)
T+0:05   → screenshot on mobile, verify rendering
T+1:00   → check view count (should be > 100 by now)
T+2:00   → post Comment 2 (engagement Q)
T+4:00   → reply to all comments received so far
T+24:00  → log metrics in /09-launch/KPI-baseline.md
```

---

## What to do if a post underperforms (< 500 views at T+2h)

1. DM 5 close connections with link, ask honest feedback
2. React on 5 niche posts with meaningful comments (not "Great post!")
3. Wait 24h before reposting variant — algo penalizes rapid republish
